Management

Fazlur Rahman

Founding Chairman


Mr. Fazlur Rahman (1947-2023) was the visionary founding chairman of City Group. Beginning his entrepreneurial journey in 1972 with the establishment of CITY OIL MILLS to produce mustard oil in Gandaria, Dhaka, Mr. Rahman faced significant challenges in a post-war economy. Despite these hurdles, he dedicated himself to producing quality mustard oil for local consumption. Under his leadership, the company rapidly grew, laying the foundation for what is now one of the most prominent business conglomerates in the country having footsteps in food product production, heavy industry, health care, private economic zone, hi-tech park, animal feed, media and communication etc. His enduring legacy continues to inspire the values and principles of City Group.
